We are Saheb Productions.

A team of crazy madcap professionals, whose passion for great storytelling, insight mining and deft execution stands second only to their passion for yummy butter chicken.

Every time you choose to shake hands with us, we call into service our rich experience of 8 years of advertising and allied services while riding on the back of 20 years of experience in production design. Confused?

Well, we are the family of Mr RP Singh, an Industry veteran of over 20 years and a much sought after name in the field of line production. Then be it, TVCs, Indian and International movies, Corporate AVs, TV Shows, Mr. RP Singh is a name that is called upon to lend any piece of work class and credibility.

We are also star makers, to our mom’s credit goes sourcing and casting for numerous Indian and International clients. Mrs. Gurmeet Kaur, has the rare skill of not just discovering new faces but also creating a talent pool that can meet the casting demands of clients of any stature.

With such a vast and diverse set of talent we can easily say that there’ll be only a few things that can catch us off guard.

  • TVC's

  • Digital Films

  • Motion Graphics

Call us crazy if you must but we like walking on paths that are hitherto unexplored. With our left-brain being the more fertile half, nothing excites us more than the prospect of cooking up something unconventional and marrying it with a unique style of execution.


Be it Television Commercials, Corporate AVs for blue chip companies, 2D and 3D animation or standalone product windows, we leave a stamp of quality that is unmistakably ours. Having worked with some a set of clients that turns our competitors green in face, we are constantly on the lookout for newer challenges. Hungry we are. Foolish we are not.
